## Local-First Architecture

We believe your data should live on your device, not trapped in someone else's cloud. Local-first architecture means your apps work offline, sync seamlessly when connected, and keep you in control. It's how software should have always been built.

The future is distributed, and Rust is the perfect fit. Its tiny binaries, zero runtime overhead, and memory safety make it ideal for edge deployments. Whether it's a Cloudflare Worker or an embedded device, Rust delivers consistent performance everywhere your code needs to run.
